Introduction

The Family Support Fund is a small fundraising initiative created among family members, especially by the four brothers, with the aim of supporting the family during financial difficulties and emergency situations. This concept was started in Chaitra 2082 with a long-term vision of building a stronger and more supportive family environment.

The main purpose of this fund is to provide immediate financial assistance whenever any family member faces emergencies such as hospitalization, medical treatment, accidents, educational needs, or other urgent situations. Instead of depending entirely on outside help during difficult times, this fund encourages family members to stand together and support one another.The fund is not only about money; it is also about strengthening relationships, building trust, and creating a culture of cooperation among family members.

Objectives of the Fund

  • To provide financial support during medical emergencies.
  • To reduce financial stress among family members.
  • To promote unity, cooperation, and mutual support.
  • To create a long-term emergency savings system for the family.
  • To encourage responsibility and regular contribution among members.

How the Fund Works

The collected amount is safely managed and can be used whenever a genuine emergency or urgent need arises within the family. Decisions regarding the use of the fund are made collectively with honesty and transparency.

Code of Conducts for this process

  1. Four brothers must contribute NPR 3,000 per month per person to the designated account.
  2. After the deposit,a payment screenshot must be shared in the group as proof of payment.
  3. If monthly payment is not possible,amount must be paid within a maximum period of 3 months.

Two major parts of Using the Fund

  1. Medical Emergency Support

    2. If any family member becomes ill and requires hospitalization, they may receive financial
    support from the fund

  2. Other than the Emergency Support

    3. In cases of emergencies other than illness like;big festivals and KUL PUJA where financial assistance is urgently needed,
    fund can be used or in the case of individual members may receive a loan from the fund.

    Repayment Terms

  • For Medical Emergency Support

    • The 40% of the amount provided will be treated as health insurance support,while the remaining
    60% must be repaid within 5 months, together with regular monthly contributions.

  • Other than the Emergency Support

    • The entire borrowed amount must be repaid within 5 months,along with regular monthly contributions.

The fund is planned to continue for many years so that future generations can also benefit from this supportive system. With proper management and cooperation, the Family Support Fund can become a strong foundation for family security and unity.

Future Vision

In the future, the Family Support Fund aims to expand its activities beyond emergency support. It may also help in educational support,welfare activities, and other family betterment programs. It aims to continue supporting family members for many generations through cooperation, transparency, and responsible financial management. The long-term dream is to create a self-sustaining support system that inspires future family members to continue the saving culture and think for others.
